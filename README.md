Plantile is a grid-based game about building your plant factory. Start from a single seed, expand your production overtime, and reach full automation!

- [Wishlist on Steam](https://store.steampowered.com/app/4565480/Plantile/)
- [Play the Demo on Itch](https://nertsal.itch.io/plantile)
- [Join the official Discord server](https://discord.gg/GRdJqB556u)

## Drone controls

A drone will help you with manual actions to kickstart your factory.
